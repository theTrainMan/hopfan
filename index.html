About the Organisation

TThe organization's main objective are to be a House of Prayer for All Nations. It’s goal is to reach the lost, disciple the saved and train the next generation of LEADERS. In doing this will mean advancing and developing a better spiritual character. This vision will lead to rebuilding a stronger community in the Kingdom of God here on the Earth..
